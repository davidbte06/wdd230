Target Audience

The target audience for this website is both people who live in Idaho, as well as people who want to visit Idaho for work or vacation. By entering this site you will be prepared for whatever the weather may be. People between the ages of 12 and 70 can log on to this website and be informed about weather forecasts as well as possible storms.

Person 1

David Burguete is a student who lives with his family in Idaho, he constantly needs to frequent the weather forecast in order to plan his days or vacations with his family. He needs a site that can meet his needs.

Person 2

Andrea Guerra is a stay at home mom who has family in idaho and from time to time goes on vacation to visit her family, many times she has not prepared for rain or snowfall because she has not checked the weather forecast. A weather forecast page could be a good way to meet those needs.

Scenarios

  • I live in Soda Springs, Idaho and there have been rumors of bad weather days coming, how can I check?
  • I am going on vacation to Preston, Idaho and would like to know the weather forecast.
  • We are planning to go skiing with my family, which days would be the best?
  • What is this week's weather forecast for Fish Haven?
  • What days of this week will it rain?
